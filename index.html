Introduction

Cryptocurrencies have shown to be the fastest growing asset class in recent years, with fantastic returns for those who chose to invest. Long-term investors and day traders both profit from the one-of-a-kind prospects provided by the most promising market we've ever seen.

To be successful, however, cryptocurrency investments require time, particular expertise, analytical abilities, and professional equipment. The difficulty is the primary reason why most people lose out on the enormous chances that a flourishing market provides.

Maneki is the platform for the profit-sharing strategies NFT, built on blockchain technology and smart contracts. We bring together strategy makers and strategy takers in a decentralized, open, and transparent network, allowing the financial market to become even more global. The platform's smart contract technology will enable an automated and completely transparent approach for profit sharing and investing.
