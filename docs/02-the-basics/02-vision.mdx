<div style={{ textAlign: "justify" }}>
  <p>
    The Maneki platform is the solution for the most crucial industry problems,
    such as lack of transparency and, as a result, a lack of trust.
  </p>
  <p>
    Maneki is about to disrupt investment management industry by launching the
    most comprehensive infrastructure for strategy makers and strategy takers
    which includes:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <p>
        <strong>Maneki Strategy NFT Platform</strong> – the essence and heart of
        the Maneki profit-sharing infrastructure. It challenges the traditional
        investment approach by using a proven peer-to-peer business model.
        Professional strategy makers will release hundreds of trading models for
        strategy takers to choose from. Each trading strategy will be specified
        as an NFT, which will allow participants to assess its present and
        historical performance. By following, the platform duplicates every
        trading activity of a selected strategy straight into investors' Maneki
        accounts, delivering a unique peer-to-peer experience while also
        ensuring the protection of assets. Takers will only pay if the model
        generates a profit for you, and all losses will be accounted for in
        order to prevent paying success fees until the profit exceeds the high
        watermark threshold.
      </p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <p>
        <strong>Manekiswap</strong> – We want to build direct connections with
        major cryptocurrency pools throughout the world. Maneki lets all users
        profit from the best possible swap and stake rates by pooling liquidity
        from leading pools. Internal liquidity is provided with greatly reduced
        price on all trading operations conducted on the Maneki Platform through
        such offers.
      </p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <p>
        <strong>Maneki Intelligence Portal</strong> – the one-stop shop for the
        most up-to-date on-chain recommendations, research, and fresh
        opportunities — everything you'll need to succeed in cryptocurrency
        investment.
      </p>
    </li>
  </ul>
</div>
